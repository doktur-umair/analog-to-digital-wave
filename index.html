üîÑ How Analog-to-Digital Conversion Works

1

Sound Waves

Your voice creates continuous pressure waves in the air that hit the microphone

2

Microphone

Converts sound pressure into continuous electrical voltage (analog signal)

3

Sampling

Measures the voltage 44,100 times per second to capture snapshots

4

Quantization

Rounds each measurement to one of 65,536 levels (16-bit resolution)

5

Binary Encoding

Converts each level to a 16-bit binary number (0s and 1s)

6

Storage

Saves the binary data to memory, hard drive, or sends over internet
